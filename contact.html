Quick Answers

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account in-app under Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will remove all your data.

Is my location data private?

Yes! Your location is only shared according to your privacy settings. Check our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the report feature in-app, or contact us directly with details about the content or user.
